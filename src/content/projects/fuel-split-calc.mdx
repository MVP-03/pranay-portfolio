# Problem

Splitting fuel costs across a multi-stop trip with several people is
tedious to do by hand and error-prone when trips change mid-route.

## Approach

Engineered and deployed a cloud-hosted full-stack application that
calculates each rider's share in real time as trip legs are added,
instead of requiring a single end-of-trip settlement.

## Outcome

A working, deployed tool that handles multi-trip cost splitting live,
removing the manual spreadsheet step entirely.
